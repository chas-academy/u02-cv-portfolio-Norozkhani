Who are we?

Welcome to Honey and Crumbs, a cozy bakery with a passion for handcrafted treats. We believe that good food brings people together, which is why every loaf, pastry, and sweet here is made with love, using simple ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Our offerings range from freshly baked sourdough and flaky croissants to whimsical cupcakes and custom pastries, each designed to add a touch of comfort and joy to your day. Whether you’re grabbing a quick treat or celebrating a special moment, we’re honored to be part of your story.

Thank you for supporting Honey and Crumbs—we can’t wait to share something delicious with you!

Some of our latest work

Here are some of the latest work done at the bakery

Freshly Baked Bread

Nothing says comfort like the aroma of fresh-baked bread. Our rustic sourdough, fluffy brioche, and crusty baguettes are made with love daily, using simple ingredients and a whole lot of patience. Perfect for every table, every day

Baked Goods Display

Step into Honey and Crumbs and be greeted by our warm, welcoming display of handcrafted delights. From golden pastries to hearty breads, our shelves are filled with treats baked to bring a little sweetness to your day

Assorted Pastries

Our pastry selection is flaky, buttery, and utterly irresistible. Choose from our signature croissants, sweet danishes, and fruit-filled turnovers—each one baked to golden perfection and ready to make your day a little sweeter

Cupcake Creations

Each cupcake is a miniature masterpiece, topped with swirls of buttercream and sprinkled with joy. Choose from classic flavors or seasonal specials, each one baked with care and decorated for a touch of whimsy
